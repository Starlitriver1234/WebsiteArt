Who is Tudor Marinescu?

Hello! My name is Tudor Marinescu. I am a young adult from Romania that has a big passion for video games, books and music. I am currently a Game Design student at the university TU Dublin.

A creator that values passion and variety

Ever since I was a child, I loved creating stories and making people happy. This is why I started all of my artistic projects! I worked on a big variety of projects. From games I made at University, to books I wrote and published myself. I even tried a bit of composing. I enjoy creating, so I like creating anything, as long as it sparks my creativity. And with dreams and determination, you can achieve anything!

I wrote quite a few novels which I published on Amazon, Google Play and Kobo, and I also composed a few albums, which are also published online. I’m a self-published author and also an indie musician. But my biggest passion has to be video games. I love creating the worlds for people to explore and making something that brings people to feel a myriad of emotions.

A hard working student

I am an alumni of the most prestigious Computer Science high school in Romania, The Gheorghe Lazar National College. And I studied for a year in the Netherlands at Breda University of Applied Sciences. I studied Creative Business there. I liked it a lot. But I always knew my true calling was video games. So I changed to TU Dublin, and have been creating games ever since.

A multi-cultural person

I am trilingual. Of course, I am native in Romanian, but I am also a certified C2 in English and a certified A2 in French. I try my best to learn to speak as many languages as possible since the more people I meet, the more perspectives I learn, and the more ideas I have for my art. This is why I also enjoy traveling a lot. I went to many places. I visited most of Europe, and I also visited some countries outside of it like Egypt and China.
