Who is Tudor Marinescu?

Hello! My name is Tudor Marinescu. I am a young adult from Romania who has a big passion for video games, books and music. I am currently a Game Design student at the university TU Dublin.

A creator that values passion and variety

Ever since I was a child, I loved creating stories and making people happy. This is why I started all of my artistic projects! I worked on a big variety of projects—from games I made at university to books I wrote and published myself. I even tried a bit of composing. I enjoy creating, so I like creating anything as long as it sparks my creativity. And with dreams and determination, you can achieve anything!

I wrote quite a few novels which I published on Amazon, Google Play and Kobo, and I also composed a few albums, which are also published online. I’m a self-published author and also an indie musician. But my biggest passion has to be video games. I love creating worlds for people to explore and making something that brings people to feel a myriad of emotions.

A hard-working student

I am an alumnus of the most prestigious Computer Science high school in Romania, The Gheorghe Lazar National College. I also studied for a year in the Netherlands at Breda University of Applied Sciences, where I studied Creative Business. I liked it a lot, but I always knew my true calling was video games. So I changed to TU Dublin and have been creating games ever since.

A multi-cultural person

I am trilingual. Of course, I am native in Romanian, but I am also certified C2 in English and certified A2 in French. I try my best to learn to speak as many languages as possible since the more perspectives I learn, the more ideas I have for my art. This is why I also enjoy travelling a lot. I have been to many places, visiting most of Europe, as well as countries like Egypt and China.
