Echoes of Redemption

My role in development: Story Board Artist, Film Editor

Echoes of Redemption is a student film that I worked on while I was a student at Breda University of Applied sciences. We were given a few weeks to make this film. We had to write the script, find actors, use the cameras, basically everything. It was a really fun and interesting experience. I learned a lot of teamwork and time management skills while working on it. It was my first ever attempt at a story board, and I’m very happy how it turned out. Me and a teammate worked on the film editing as well. I provided a rough edit in which I made the transitions and placed the clips in order and she then did the effects and touched it up to look more professional. Being on a set was an amazing experience that will be hard to forget!
