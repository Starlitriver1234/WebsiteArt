Books

Welcome! Here you will find info about all of the books that I have written as of this time. Some of them are in Romanian and some are in English.

I am a self published author and I started writing when I was 13 with my first book: Dino and The Diamond Star(Book is in Romanian). I wanted to explore the feelings that people experience every day through writing and adventures. I published this book when I was 14 in 2020 on Google Play. The Dino Trilogy was my first ever series, and I loved writing it a lot. It later got a translation in English that contained all three books, simply named Dino. All of the art, editing, writing, publishing is done by me. All of my books have an ISBN from the National Library of Romania as well. Since I was a child, I wanted to make the best stories I could and I was very ambitious.

After Dino concluded, I wrote my first ever book in English while also ironically studying for an English exam. Moonlit Rain. It was also my first ever book in first person and also first ever romance. It was a joy to write and I think it encapsulates that high school charm in a rural town the same way Dino encapsulated the wonder in all our childhoods. This book explored many themes, from anxiety, to finding friends in the most unlikely of places. It is set in a charming little village in Germany and has a cosy atmosphere. The focus was pulled back from the plot and remained mainly on the arcs of the characters and their feelings. To this day, it is one of my favourite books that I have written just because of how pleasant the world in it is.

Once I published Moonlit Rain in 2022, and it received some updates that added more content, I focused on my next book. Kaleo and Hayden. This is one of my most ambitious projects to date. It took three years to write and is my longest book. It is also a romance. But the focus is more on the healing of the characters through their relationship and on philosophical questions like the role of tradition in the modern world. It is set in a fantasy country and explores the themes of friendship, love, people pleasing, anxiety, overworking and grief. The book is both a charming and cozy read but also a powerful one as the arcs are more serious and emotionally impactful. This book also got a second version that removed the artwork for people who would prefer to only have the text.

And now we have reached my latest book! Celestial Forest. Originally created as a Twine game for University, this little project became an extremely fun and beautiful adventure with a theme of Halloween. It focuses on the difficulties of dealing with past failures and moving to another country while also presenting an adorable romance. Set in a haunted forest, the book presents lots of mini adventures as the characters unravel a mystery, grow and eventually fall in love. The forest is a representation of being stuck in your own head, and the main theme of the book is how human connection ccan help us grow and escape it. This is also the most comical of my books with the characters being more sarcastic and funny.

Books in English

Celestial Forest

Welcome to Verdant Island, the most welcoming and friendly country there is! You can never be an outsider here! At least that's what Elwyn thought when he arrived. In reality, it isn’t that easy, and he feels more like an outsider than he ever did. Foreign words, weird signs and customs. Everything is different and strange.

One day, finally having had enough and needing some time to think, he goes to a nearby forest called The Celestial Forest. It all starts beautifully, with him reading peacefully under the cover of the trees as birds sing and the breeze ruffles his hair. But… It was not to last…

Suddenly trapped in a cursed forest full of horrors, Elwyn must face his fears and find the courage in his heart if he wants to survive. Terrified and alone, he stumbles through the dark till he meets... Blake, an eccentric musician and actor with a heart of gold, piercing green eyes, and so much muscle he seems unreal. The two quickly bond and create a magical friendship that could turn into even more. But will they manage to survive? Can their bond resist these horrors? Will they ever escape? Dive into this Halloween-inspired romance with quirky characters and beautiful imagery that will wow you, and find out! After all, what is in the dark is always a mystery.

Fated Spirits - Kaleo and Hayden

In a time that feels almost like an old memory, Kaleo was the heart of his community. A young man with a sporty and laid-back personality. A bit quirky, but also very kind, helping anyone in need. Now, he’s trapped by an old tradition that made him vanish from the world. Unseen and unheard, Spirit Kaleo can only watch his old life from afar. The only thing on his mind being hope that one day, someone will see him once more and make him feel loved and understood.

Enter Hayden. A determined and hardworking painter with a passion for history. His only wish? To be recognised for his art. But his entire life is about to be uprooted as crumbling debt knocks at his door. In a desperate move to save his career, Hayden enters the forbidden forest of the spirit. His journey takes an unexpected turn when a deadly fall nearly claims his life. Thankfully, Kaleo manages to save him just in time. But the moment they’re out of danger, something extraordinary happens. His powers vanish, and he becomes temporarily human once more. The two quickly bond and discover a connection like neither thought they’d ever have.

Brought together by fate, the two will now face many challenges. From insecurity and past events to the strict rules that forbid people from entering the forest. But none of it matters, as long as they have each other. Still, one question will need to be answered. Will love be able to save them both?

Moonlit Rain

Noah is shy and easily scared, always hiding away his true self. But everything changes on a rainy night as the 18-year-old boy meets Rafael, a new classmate who loves to cook and play the guitar. His beautiful looks, kind personality and amusing laughter bring winds of change in Noah's life, who, for the first time falls in love. The two quickly become friends as they share their passions and hobbies, playing music together or cooking delicious baked goods. The two can never be apart as their friendship grows and blossoms and hopefully, it might even turn into something more. Joined by their friends, Selena and Andrew, the four embark on a journey of love and discovery as they understand each other better and mature as well. They face many challenges but united, no one can stand in their way!

Dino

Be by his side as he moves to the Muzicalo Kingdom, a land of wonder and fun, where magic toys and books live together with people in harmony. What follows is an amazing adventure of self-discovery and magic as Dino tries to uncover the secrets of the magical kingdom, meeting friends and foes alike! Join Dino on his magical adventure through mysterious and unknown lands! From beautiful songs to amazing sights, Dino will see it all! Fly through the clouds and visit floating castles, walk through jungles covered in snow, or visit a beautiful underwater city! Adventure through the unknown and meet a variety of fun characters! And most importantly, uncover the secrets of the Muzicalo Kingdom along with Dino and his friends! Also! Have fun!
